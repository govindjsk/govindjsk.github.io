Govind Sharma

Senior Data Scientist

Reed.co.uk, London, UK

Information Retrieval, Machine Learning, Deep Learning / Neural Networks, Network Science, Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Mathematics, Causality, Graph Neural Networks, Hypergraph Neural Networks


I am a senior data scientist at Reed.co.uk, London, UK, where my team works on applying AI -- Information Retrieval (Search/Recommendation Engines), and text data mining and analysis -- to the recruitment-tech domain. Prior to this, I was a postdoctoral research fellow at the University of Surrey with Dr. Yunpeng Li, where our focus was on designing and developing machine-learning tools for automated dental disease detection from radiographs.

In 2020, I had completed my PhD in Machine Learning and its applications in Hypergraph Networks from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, India, supervised by Prof. M. Narasimha Murty.
