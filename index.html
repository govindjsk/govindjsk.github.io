I am a data scientist at Factors.Ai, where we work on developing cutting edge tools that generate intelligent marketing insights. Before that, I had completed my PhD from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, India, supervised by Prof. M. Narasimha Murty. My primary research focuses on Machine/Deep Learning approaches to Social and Information Networks, Higher-order Relations (esp., Hypergraphs), and Natural Language Processing.
